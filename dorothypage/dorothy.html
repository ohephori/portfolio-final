The Mission

Project Overview

For this project I worked with a person named Dorothy to create her a one page website for her portfolio. This project included making sections for her about section, portfolio, and contact information.

Project Role

My role for this project was lead designer. I was to create her website from stage a to z and implement any recommendations she had for her site.

The Problem & Goal for the Project

The problem for this project was to make sure I was advertising towards her suggested audience, aka museums and magazine publications.
The goal was to make sure Dorothy's advertising goals were met, while creating a sleek and unique design for her site.

The Process

Digital Wireframe

In this mockup I laid out her basic elements to make sure spacing and equality was instilled in every section.

Low-Fidelity Comp

This mockup is where I added her text, navigation, and contact information. I did this to ensure I was prioritizing space and style for her most important elements.

High-Fidelity Comp

The last mockup is where I finally implemented her colors, pictures, information, and final typography. Some minor tweaks were made to things like the footer and logo to make sure aesthetic was met.

Final Color Scheme and Typography

The Final Design

Challenges & Takeaways

After many iterations, and Dorothy's input, I was able to implement her wanted changes and come full circle with her design. Not only that but I was able to make her site intuitive enough to implement any future changes she may want to add, whether it is pictures, contact info or other.
I took away from this project a new sense of communication skills and ingenuity in design. Dorothy helped me try out a new style of website design and gave great feedback that will help me with my future projects.

Project Conclusion

In conclusion, this project turned out to be a fun one. I learned a lot about design and reiterating clients opinions. Coming out of this project with new skills in design, communication, and above all else a solid foundation in the design process.
