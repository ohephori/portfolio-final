The Mission

Project Overview

I was commissioned to create a design for Joe's music portfolio. He asked for a simplistic, sleek, and dark design to compliment his music style with a short about and contact section.

Project Role

My role in this project was to be head designer for Joe's website. I was to create a basic website to showcase his music using music players along with other aspects.

The Problem & Goal for the Project

The main problem for this project was making sleek music players that matched the client's need for a sophisticated yet dark design and theme.
The main goal for this project was to make sure the clients aesthetic needs were met while also properly showcasing their skills and work.

The Process

Digital Wireframe

I started off laying out the pages main elements. Showing where each photos, paragraph, header, copyright, music players etc. would be generally placed.

Low-Fidelity Comp

I brought in the main text for each section and reevaluated the positioning of some aspects. I also placed in social media icons and copyright to make sure spacing was correct and looking good.

High-Fidelity Comp

With everything else in place I brought in the about image of Joe as well as the album arts. With some more little tweaks to the positioning the final design was ready. I showed Joe the high fidelity mockup and he was super satisfied with the product and had no more input.

Final Color Scheme and Typography

The Final Design

Challenges & Takeaways

Like mentioned before, a big challenge was creating the music players for Joe's site. I didn't find too much else to be challenging with this project. The client was straightforward with their wants and needs and ended up liking the initial style that I decided to go for.
I took away from this project new skills in design and a lot of good communication skills after talking to and working with a new client.

Project Conclusion

In conclusion this project was by far one of my favorites to create. The client and I worked hand in hand to create the perfect site for his needs.
